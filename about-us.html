About Us

Prince Mega Agency a formerly known as PRINCE TRUST AGENCY, is a wholly indigenous cosmetics and beauty company. It started operations in 1998 as a small sole proprietorship business at Creek Road, Borikiri, trading under the name PRINCE TRUST AGENCY. The business gained popularity as it moved to the store in Mile 1 market (POWA PLAZA), Diobu. PRINCE TRUST AGENCY adopted the name PRINCE MEGA AGENCY and became incorporated in 2007. Over the past 21 years, PRINCE MEGA AGENCY has grown from a small business in a shop, into a Group of Companies with 5 Branches and a very large warehouse with particular interest in Body care products, Baby care products, Make-Up Products, Designer perfumes, Toiletries, and General Cosmetics.

Achieving a sustainable Global Standard has remained the driving force of our business over these years and has led to the company becoming a key distributors in RIVERS STATE to Major Brands within and outside Nigeria, amongst which is PROCTER AND GAMBLE (the manufacturers of Oral-B, Pampers, Always, Gillette, and Safeguard), Fair & White, Emos Best, Fragrance World, Zaron, Black up, Zikel, and many others listed on our partners page. We have built a strong customer relationship with over 500 Supermarkets and Pharmaceuticals across Nigeria as well as a thriving business patronage from over 100 wholesalers nationwide.

At PRINCE MEGA AGENCY we have maintained a standard of Excellence and Integrity thereby making our products and services stand out as the best in terms of Quality, Affordability and Customer Care Services.
